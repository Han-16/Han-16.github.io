About

I am a master's student at Kookmin University EECS advised by Prof. Jihye Kim. My research interests lie in the intersection of cryptography and blockchain technology. I specialize in applied cryptography, with a particular focus on SNARKs and privacy-preserving AI. I'm passionate about leveraging cryptography to solve real-world problems in trust, integrity, and data privacy.
